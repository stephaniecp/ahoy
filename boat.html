About

The Boat is a Tanzer 7.5 (stands for 7.5 meters, so just under 25 feet), built in 1978. She is a sloop and was designed by Johann Tanzer. The 7.5 model was designed with two different keel, the fin keel and shoal keel (the one Strangewaves has), with only 2.67ft draft. For motor she is equipped with a 9.9 horse power Yahama outbord from 2012.
